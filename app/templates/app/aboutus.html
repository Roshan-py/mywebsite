About

Labhesshh has been amongst top wooden cold pressed oil manufacturers in Raigad Maharashtra, Since 2019.We extract oils from various seeds using wooden churner(Lakdi ghana/ kachi ghani) we rely on 100% chemical free oil extraction process. Traditional wooden ghani process helps oil to retain its aroma, taste and nutritional values which refined oil fails to provide. filtration is done manually with atmost hygiene The amount of oil needed for cooking is comparatively less as compared to refined oil. Our vision is to promot healthy food habits to everyone.
